Fascicles

Projected List of Fascicles

The primary focus of the project is the production of the Etymological Dictionary and Thesaurus, organized by semantic fields. The first volume of the Dictionary is Body part Nomenclature, which will be published in smaller units called fascicles. The etymologies belonging to the first fascicle, relating to reproductive anatomy, will be published electronically starting in 2004.

The current projection for the entire series is as follows:

Volume 1 Body Parts
Volume 2 Animals
Volume 3 Natural Objects, Plants, Foods
Volume 4 Kinship Terms, Ethnonyms, Social Roles
Volume 5 Culture, Artifacts, Religion
Volume 6 Verbs of Motion, of Manipulation, and of Production
Volume 7 Adjectival Verbs
Volume 8 Abstract Nouns and Verbs, Psychological Verbs, Verbs of Utterance
Volume 9 Shape, Size, Color, Measure, Number, Time, Space
Volume 10 Grammatical words

Other Publications

In addition to the main volumes, STEDT also publishes a monograph series. Descriptions and order information is available on the STEDT publications page.
